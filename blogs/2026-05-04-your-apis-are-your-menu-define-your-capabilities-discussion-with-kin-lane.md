Your APIs Are Your Menu, Define Your Capabilities - Discussion with Kin Lane Mon, 05/04/2026 - 15:00 We welcome Kin Lane, API Evangelist and co-founder of Naftiko. This episode encourages enterprises to publish their “API menu” so others can clearly see what they offer, while recognizing that true competitive advantage lies in the quality behind that menu. The discussion emphasizes the need for organizations to first take inventory of, and clearly define, their capabilities in order to deliver real business outcomes, rather than simply chasing the latest technical trends. It also highlights the importance of bridging the semantic gap through well-aligned documentation, helping prevent AI systems from misrepresenting or hallucinating a company’s offerings. Bio Kin Lane Chief Community Officer & Co-founder at Naftiko. Known globally as the API Evangelist, Kin has spent over a decade shaping API standards, governance, and developer experience. His work at Postman and Bloomberg pairs with his research on OpenAPI, AsyncAPI, and JSON Schema… all now converging into Naftiko's open-source core.
